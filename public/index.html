How it works

  • Resizes images to 500x500 pixels or smaller
  • Compresses to 512 KB or less
  • Keeps compressing until both conditions are met
  • Supports PNG, JPG, and animated GIF files
  • GIFs are processed frame-by-frame to preserve animation
